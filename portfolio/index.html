About me

I build systems
that actually work.

I am a Computer Science student at Kampala International University passionate about building scalable digital systems that combine clean engineering, intuitive design, and practical real-world functionality.

My work focuses on full-stack web and mobile development using modern technologies such as React, Flutter, Supabase, PostgreSQL, and Tailwind CSS. I enjoy architecting systems from the ground up — from database structure and backend logic to frontend user experience and workflow optimization.

Alongside software engineering, I specialize in football analytics and technical writing. I produce tactical analysis, scouting reports, and long-form football breakdowns using advanced metrics such as xG, xA, PAdj, and possession-chain analysis. This combination of analytical thinking and software development shapes how I approach problem-solving: structured, data-driven, and user-focused.
